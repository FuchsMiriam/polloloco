The story of Pepe Peligroso

Pepe Peligroso, a fearless farmer of Mexico, woke up to a nightmare: his prized chickens had escaped! These weren’t ordinary chickens; they were feisty, fearless, and had a knack for mischief. Armed with only a sombrero and salsa bottles, Pepe embarked on a daring mission to reclaim his runaway flock. As Pepe reached the cornfield, he saw them—his chickens, led by the notorious Cluck Norris. The chickens had formed a rowdy circle, clucking like a mariachi band. Pepe knew he had to act fast. With swift throws of his salsa bottles, he shouted, “¡Vuelvan a la granja!” But Cluck Norris wasn’t backing down. The lead chicken squared off, flapping his wings like a prizefighter. And so the fight began!
